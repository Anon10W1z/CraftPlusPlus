Welcome

Welcome to Craft++'s website! This website contains the latest downloads, information, news and more for Craft++. Feel free to take a look around!

Introduction

Craft++ is a mod designed to enhance vanilla Minecraft without going overboard. It is designed to be minimally invasive and configurable to create a user experience like none other.

Features

Craft++ adds a multitude of features to enhance your world. Warning for a wall of text:

  • Storage blocks for flint, sugar, and charcoal. These act exactly like vanilla storage blocks. Sugar storage blocks have gravity, and charcoal blocks can be used like coal blocks in a furnace.
  • Falling blocks such as sand, sugar, and gravel can be fired out of dispensers. Great for suffocation traps!
  • Enhanced mob drops! Named mobs will drop their name tags. Bats drop leather and endermen drop their held blocks. Finally, creepers have a 1/10 chance of dropping a TNT block.
  • More realistic predator/prey relationships! When ocelots kill chickens, the chickens won't drop meat! Same for wolves and sheep!
  • Players can throw ender pearls in creative mode, just like in Xbox!
  • Creepers and baby zombies burn in daylight. Wander far and near in comfort and safety!
  • Players can shear name tags right off mobs.
  • Seeds will plant themselves after a (randomly-determined) couple of seconds delay. Farms can now be more easily automated!
  • When hovering over mob spawners with your mouse in creative mode, you can see the name of the mob it spawns.
  • Mob spawners drop themselves when using silk touch!
  • Active potion effect icons are displayed in the top left corner!
  • Seeds/stems don't sound like stone anymore when you break them.
  • Fire no longer sounds like wool if you jump over it! (Seriously, why was that even there in the first place?)
  • Command blocks can be found in the redstone creative tab.
  • Mob spawners can be found in the miscellaneous creative tab.
  • Buttons are named according to their material! (E.g. stone/wooden buttons)
  • Paper can be fished! I wonder what it says...
  • Stone tools are crafted out of stone rather than cobblestone.
  • You only need 3, rather than 6, source blocks to craft stairs. They are still crafted in the same pattern.
  • Crafting buttons gives you 4 rather than 1.
  • Lastly (finally), wool can be back-crafted into 4 string.
